## Visual DNA

Deep darkness with warm gold. Background is near-black (#0A0A0E) com superfícies em dark navy (#0D1117). Gold (#B59A5B) como único accent: flat, matte, antigo. Zero shadows. Zero glow. Herdado do Genesis OS da Bethel Educação.

## Colors

Dark mode: background #0A0A0E (Preto Elite), surfaces #0D1117 (Dark Navy), #141820, #1A1F28. Texto primário #E8E0D4 (parchment quente), secundário #999999 (Cinza Médio), faint #5E564E. Gold: #B59A5B (principal), #D4B97A (claro/hover), #8C7640 (escuro). Active = gold wash 12% opacity.

Light mode: #FFFFFF (white), surfaces #F7F7F5/#F0EFEC. Texto #1A1A1A, secundário #555555. Gold escurece para #8C7640.

## Constraints

No shadows. No glow. No gradients on UI. Active = gold bg wash. Inputs 20px radius. Lora for headlines only. Body ≤15px.
